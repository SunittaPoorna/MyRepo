ABOUT JUEGO STUDIO

Request a Quote

Juego Studios is a leading game development company, creating high quality cross platform games for iOS, Android, Windows mobile, Desktops & Facebook. We are a team of 50+ with our headquarters & development center in Bangalore. We also have Art Design center and sales offices in Miami & London. We are one of the fastest growing game development companies. With our effective game development process and unique mode of approach in Game-Design, Artwork, Development & Testing, we aim to reach pinnacle in Gaming Industry.

At Juego Studios we have a vision to develop high quality games by using latest tools & technologies to become a world-class game development studio. We are a team of young, motivated, energetic & passionate professionals committed to success. We work closely with our clients to deliver beyond their expectations. Our analytical skills, development expertise and Industry exposure have started to earn us a globally acknowledged reputation in the game development technology space within just a few years of coming into existence.

Facts:

  • We were founded in 2011 as an LLP.
  • In 2013 we Registered has Private Limited Company.
  • Juego Studios got ISO 9001:2008 Quality Certification.
  • We have developed 50 + Games and Apps for our clients & partners which have been downloaded by millions of users.

Our Skills

The team at Juego Studios exhibits a variety of skills in terms of both Technical & Creative aspects. We have been recognized due to our remarkable skills in cross platform game development. We excel in programming languages like C, C++, Objective C, Java, C#, JavaScript, LAMP, etc. Our technical team has expertise in Cross platform Game-Engines like Unity3D, Cocos2D-X, Corona etc. with deep insight knowledge in OpenGL/ ES 2.0+ & Shaders. Our creative team is specialized in Art, Graphic Design, UX/UI design, 3D Modeling, Animation, Video Trailers and much more.
